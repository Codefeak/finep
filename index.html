BACKGROUND

The team behind Finep Engineering has been working with hydro- and solarpower development for many years. In 2007, solarpower systems for the Kenyan and Ghanan market were developed. In 2014, solar systems were developed for the Nepalese market. It helped build up a good business network which resulted in an interest to develop hydropower in Nepal. In 2018, a new company was founded with the purpose to explore and pursue developments of renewable energy and civil engineering projects in Nepal, Ghana and Peru. Finep Engineering is currently focusing on development of hydropower projects with focus on electro-mechanical equipment in Nepal, and civil engineering projects in Nordic regions.

CORPORATE GOVERNANCE

CORPORATE GOVERNANCE

To ensure a basis of an effective corporate governance framework, Finep Engineering promote transparency, fair markets and efficient allocation of resources and acts only in the rule of law and support effective supervision and enforcement. Finep Engineering follows ILO Declaration on Fundamental Principles and Rights at Work and The Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR). To achieve a sound corparate governance, Finep Engineering strives to uphold the following five principles:


1. Good business ethics

Finep Engineering company´s executives should be honest and truthful in all their business doings, in all their business activities. They show personal integrity and will fight for their beliefs no matter the situation or pressure. Executives must strive to be honorable and upright no matter the circumstances; be trustworthy and just in their business dealings and never use or disclose information learned in confidence for personal advantage; accomplish the business objectives in a way that causes the least harm and the greatest positive good; respect human dignity, autonomy, privacy, rights, and interests of all those who have a stake in their decisions; treat all people with equal respect regardless of sex, race or national origin; follow the law, rules and regulations related to their business activities; create an environment in which ethical decision making is valued; protect and build the company’s good reputation and the morale of its employees.

2. Aligned business goals

Finep Engineering aims to target business goals which reflects the expectations of all it's investors. This is accomplished in particular through continuous dialogues with Finep Engineering´s investors, but also with it´s partners and staff.


3. Strategic Management

Finep Engineering is organised and run according to the following rules:

  • To find and complete hydropower projects with focus on electro-mechanical equipment and to explore and complete civil engineering projects.

  • To develop, maintain and supervise projects by cost- efficient use of broad technical resources.

4. Organisational effectiveness

Finep Engineering is a functional organisation.


5. Reporting

Finep Engineering believes that improvements of the organisational effectivenes is an ongoing process, that consists of modifications of the organisational form and style through regular consultations with it´s stakeholders and partners.

ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY

ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY

Finep Engineering´s Environmental Policies, EP, have been implemented to raise awareness and educate the public and industry of different environmental impacts that hydropower can have on the environment, on the community.


VISION

Finep Engineering strives to develop hydropower with as little effect as possible on the following environmental values (EVs):

  • drinking water supply;

  • aquatic ecosystems;

  • primary industries, (including irrigation, stock watering, aquaculture, human consumers of aquatic foods, general farm use);

  • recreational use (swimming, boating, visual recreation);

  • industrial water;

  • cultural and spiritual values.
